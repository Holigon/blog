Who am I?

My name is Noah, and I'm 20 years old. I was raised in Denmark, and at 3 years old my facination with computers started. Since then I've helped numerous members of the public (read: my family, and friends) with their computer problems. Though restarting your grandmother's computer for the 900th time does make the IT field a little stale, so I decided to learn something new.

I tried my hand at programming quite a while ago, but life got in the way, and I never got furthur with it. I aim to change that this time. I'm teaching myself Python at the moment, and am mastering the fundamentals - once again - after my hiatus

My hope is to master Python, and then start joining open source projects to collaborate with people, to gain experience in the field of programming.
I'm looking forward to updating this page again, since that could mean that some of my goals have been achieved!
